    1. Your ADR (Average Daily Rate) is $56.

Note that this may change at a later date, and thus you should incorporate some way of changing it afterwards into your spreadsheet model.
    3. Energy costs are estimated at 5% of revenue.
    4. Administration expenses are fixed at $25,000 per month.
    5. Each month, you must pay TVA of 20% on the previous months sales.
